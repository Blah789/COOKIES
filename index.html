Snickerdoodles

Recipe

Step 1 Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Step 2 Cream together butter, shortening, 1 1/2 cups sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Blend in flour, cream of tartar, soda, and salt. Shape dough by rounded spoonfuls into balls. Step 3 Mix 2 tablespoons sugar and cinnamon together. Roll balls of dough in mixture. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets. Step 4 Bake 8 to 10 minutes, or until set but not too hard. Remove immediately from baking sheets.
